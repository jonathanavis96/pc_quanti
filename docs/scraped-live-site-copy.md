## SERVICES PAGE (/services)

### Page Title
PC quanti - Services

### Page Heading
Our Services

### Introduction
We have extensive experience in various contract forms including NEC3, NEC4, FIDIC, GCC, JBCC and deploy services in project management, engineering management, planning, construction management, Quantity Surveying, cost, value and risk management, planning, contract and claims management, dispute resolution, adjudication and arbitration, with a proven track record of delivering complex major EPC projects.

### Service: Project Management
PC Quanti provide turnkey project management services to suit your project needs, from initial design and engineering development phase, through on-site execution and project close-out phases. We can also provide services to existing projects by bolstering your existing team, where we guarantee that we will add value to your project team with our expertise. We Implement programme management frameworks, integrating schedule development, risk management, change control and key client and stakeholder risks and interface management to manage time, cost and scope delivery, ensuring issue resolution and alignment between project objectives and contractual obligations.

### Service: Outage Management
We have extensive experience in planning plant outage shutdown turnarounds, where 24-hour per day, 7 days per week working is required to deliver the works in the shortest possible timeframe. We manage multiple stakeholder interfaces, operational constraints and activity preparation, readiness reviews, execution control and close-out processes to ensure outage milestones are achieved within the defined timeframe and budget requirements. We coordinate engineering, procurement, construction and commercial interfaces to ensure efficient works delivery.

### Service: Planning
Extensive experience providing structured and strategic planning services on various forms of contract, most notability engineering planning to facilitate the execution of NEC, FIDIC, GCC contracts. We support both clients and contractors in developing a strategic level of planning and project controls. Our strategic planning foundation enables us to actively manage project delivery, support extension of time claims, and perform delay analysis.

**Operational Project Planning:**
- Developing and managing the basis of schedule, for example using schedule guide GAO 16 89G, best practices for project schedules.
- Activity resourcing / resource loading and implementing data tracking and reporting tools
- Critical Path Method (CPM): identification the critical path, which determines the project's minimum duration.
- Float (Total/Free): "float" or "slack" is very common to understand schedule flexibility.
- Baseline: creating, updating, and comparing project baselines for progress tracking.
- Resource Loading/Allocation: Assigning labour, equipment, and material resources to activities.
- Earned Value Management (EVM): S-curve, Actual Cost (ACWP), Planned Value (BCWS), and Earned Value (BCWP).
- Look-ahead Schedules: Creating 3-week or 3-month schedules to focus on near-term work
- Import/Export (XER/Excel): Tips on importing and exporting project data, particularly using .xer files and Excel

**Daily Claims:**
We provide services to both clients and contractors to manage delays using recognised forensic schedule analysis techniques to ensure we remain compliant with recognised Time Impact Analysis standards.
- Windows Analysis / Time Slice Windows: Retrospective, step-by-step analysis.
- As-Planned vs. As-Built Analysis: A fundamental and straightforward comparison method.
- Collapsed As-Built (But-For Analysis): A common retrospective method to determine what would have happened "but for" the delays.
- Impacted As-Planned: A "what-if" scenario often searched for early-stage project analysis.

### Service: Construction Management
Managing the interfaces, risks and tight schedules requires a diligent Construction Manager to lead your works implementation on site. We have a proven track record of delivering the most complex projects in the most challenging environments, such as managing multidisciplinary complex execution in operational nuclear and petrochemical plants. We deploy thorough systems to ensure your project is executed professionally, and concisely, ensuring your company's interests are protected. Whether you require management of the main contractor work or certain subcontractors, or require a client-side team to manage the project for you, we can provide you with the necessary services.

### Service: Contract Administration
We believe that for a project to be truly successful it is critical to implement proper and effective contract administration. We ensure concise but comprehensive written project communication, serving the required notices on time, and properly documenting the progress of the works, commercial administration required under the contract form, and work with established document controls and EDMS systems to ensure your important project documentation is stored safely.

### Service: Contract Management
We provide comprehensive Contract Management support throughout the lifecycle of construction and engineering projects. Our services include advisory on contractual compliance, risk allocation, payment certification, variation management, extension of time assessments, and the utilisation of the various forms of procurement and forms of construction contracts.

We assist employers, contractors, and professional teams in administering contracts in a manner that is commercially sound, procedurally compliant, and defensible in the event of a dispute. By ensuring clear record-keeping, structured decision-making, and timely contractual notices, we help mitigate risk and reduce the likelihood of disputes arising.

Our approach is proactive and detail-driven, with a focus on protecting our clients' commercial interests while maintaining project momentum.

### Service: Quantity Surveying
Administering the contract is supported by detailed quantity surveying, from performing variation analysis and compensation event quotation building or assessment to managing the payment assessment process. Our quantity surveying services dovetail with the work in progress and contract management to ensure value is maintained. We also provide quantity surveying services in assessing claims, preparing claims, disputes and arbitrations.

### Service: Commercial Management
We provide full commercial management of projects to manage risks and maximise profitability. Our services include procurement planning and execution, estimating, tendering and tender analysis, award management, project cost / value analysis and reporting, risk management, overseeing design development and value engineering, overall time management and generally coordinating the project resources to achieve the commercial success of the project.

### Service: Risk Management
PC quanti provides specialised Risk Management services tailored to the construction and engineering sector. We help developers, contractors and project stakeholders identify, assess and mitigate potential risks before they impact timelines, budgets or compliance. From project feasibility and cost exposure analysis to contractual, operational and site-based risk evaluation, our structured approach ensures proactive decision-making and improved project certainty. By combining technical expertise with real-world industry experience, PC Quanti enables clients to minimise financial loss, manage uncertainty effectively, and deliver projects with greater confidence and control.

### Service: Dispute Resolution, Adjudication and Arbitration
We aim to avoid disputes and manage your project around this core principle. Should a dispute be unavoidable we provide specialist advice and representation in construction and engineering disputes across all formal and alternative dispute resolution forums. Our services include strategic and practical guidance on contractual dispute resolution procedures, the preparation of statements of claim, statements of defence and counterclaims for adjudication and arbitration proceedings, and comprehensive arbitration support from referral through to final award.

We assist clients with the formulation and presentation of construction-related claims, evidentiary preparation, and the coordination of expert input to ensure technically robust and commercially persuasive submissions. We also support and lead dispute resolution negotiations and draft clear, enforceable settlement agreements designed to achieve efficient and sustainable outcomes.

## PROJECTS PAGE (/projects)

### Page Title
PC quanti - Projects

### Page Heading
Our Projects

### Introduction
PC Quanti has extensive experience delivering and supporting complex engineering and construction projects across power generation, oil & gas, and major infrastructure sectors. We work alongside employers, contractors, and consultants throughout the full project lifecycle, providing disciplined technical, commercial, and contractual oversight to ensure projects are properly planned, managed, and controlled. From early-stage development through execution, completion, and close-out, PC Quanti brings clarity to cost, programme, risk, and contractual obligations — enabling informed decision-making and supporting successful project outcomes in demanding, high-value environments locally and internationally.

### Project: PTR Tanks Project, 20-year life extension, Koeberg Nuclear Power Station
As part of a programme of upgrades required for the license extension by the South African Nation Nuclear Regulator, our team managed the engineering and on-site implementation of this iconic project on the Koeberg site, providing Project Management, Contract and Construction Management, Project Controls and Planning Management under an NEC Option A contract with the utility, to successful implement this first of a kind nuclear project.

The works entailed constructing two borated water storage tanks for the reactor cavity and spent fuel cooling system in SAF2205 Duplex Stainless Steel under the ASME III code, with each weld subject to 100% radiography (X-ray). The team successfully managed and coordinated the works on the Koeberg Site, using a 500t crawler crane to lift the tanks into their final position and integrate the many complex piping and other services connections into the plant.

### Project: Steam Generator Project, 20-year life extension, Koeberg Nuclear Power Station
As part of a programme of upgrades required for the license extension by the South African Nation Nuclear Regulator, PC quanti provided a comprehensive team to manage the on-site implementation works of this iconic project on the Koeberg Operating Unit site, providing Project Management, Contract and Construction Management, Subcontractor management and coordination, Project Controls and Planning Management to the French specialist OEM, under an NEC3 Option A contract.

The works which took place during outages of the respective operating units entailed removing the original steam generators, 3 per unit, and handling and installing new steam generators weighing approximately 350t each. Our experienced team proved invaluable in the execution of this complex project that took several years to engineer, plan, and implement, involving substantial logistics, heavy lifting, major welding scope and multiple other activities in a first of a kind operation on African soil.
