# Project: PC_quanti
Purpose: Professional website for nuclear/industrial project management consultancy targeting UK B2B market
Tech Stack: Next.js, React, Tailwind CSS, TypeScript
Location: pc_quanti

## Detailed Description

PC quanti is a boutique construction consultancy providing project delivery resourcing and coordination services for industrial projects (primarily nuclear sector, expanding to other industries). Operating as a lean core-plus-associates model, they source and manage specialists (project managers, quantity surveyors, planners, legal) on demand to augment client teams.

**Target Market:** B2B companies in the UK (priority) and South Africa. Decision-makers within industrial/engineering firms seeking project management, QS, and delivery support.

**Current State:** No website, low online profile, referred via word-of-mouth. Need to establish credibility and "exist" online when prospects search.

**Brand:** Simple, professional. Logo: bright blue (#2C02D9) house icon with "PC quanti | PROJECT SERVICES" wordmark. Client prefers clean, crisp, non-frilly design (reference: HattinghPC.co.za style over CBEC's "lumpy boxes").

Goals: Discovery & design (logo, sitemap, messaging, copy), core website build (home, about, services, contact), technical setup (hosting, email, SEO), content & marketing (LinkedIn, lead gen), polish & launch

## Detailed Goals

1. **Phase 0 - Discovery & Design:**
   - Finalize logo/brand assets and color system
   - Define sitemap, messaging architecture, and content strategy
   - Create design system (Tailwind config, typography, components)
   - Write or approve all copy (services, about, sectors, value props)

2. **Phase 1 - Core Website Build:**
   - Home page: hero, services overview, credibility signals, strong CTAs
   - About page: team structure, operating model, differentiators
   - Services page: detailed service descriptions (PM, QS, planning, resourcing)
   - Sectors page (optional): industries served (nuclear, commercial, industrial)
   - Contact page: enquiry form with service dropdown/checklist

3. **Phase 2 - Technical Setup:**
   - Domain & hosting setup (pcquanti.co.za)
   - Professional email migration from Afrihost → new host, Outlook-compatible
   - SEO fundamentals (meta tags, schema markup, sitemap, robots.txt)
   - Analytics setup (Google Analytics 4 or privacy-first alternative)

4. **Phase 3 - Content & SEO:**
   - Case studies/projects page (if approved by client—NDA constraints may limit this)
   - Blog foundation for thought leadership (optional, future phase)
   - LinkedIn integration prep (social share tags, company page link)

5. **Phase 4 - Marketing & Lead Gen:**
   - Enquiry form optimization and email notifications
   - Optional: lead magnet (e.g., "Guide to Sourcing Scarce Nuclear Skills")
   - Social media strategy (LinkedIn priority for B2B, Instagram secondary)
   - Monthly social content creation & scheduling (via ReachRight Marketing add-on)

6. **Phase 5 - Polish & Launch:**
   - Cross-browser/device testing
   - Accessibility audit (WCAG 2.1 AA)
   - Performance optimization (Core Web Vitals)
   - Soft launch → feedback → adjustments → public launch

## Success Criteria

- Website live and indexed by Google within 2 weeks of launch
- Professional email operational in Outlook
- Enquiry form functional and delivering leads to info@pcquanti.co.za
- Mobile-responsive, fast (<3s load), accessible
- Brand consistency across all pages and touchpoints
- Client can update simple content (or we provide monthly updates via subscription)

## Technical Requirements

- **Framework:** Next.js 14+ (App Router)
- **Styling:** Tailwind CSS with custom theme (brand colors, typography)
- **Hosting:** Vercel or similar (auto-deploy from git)
- **CMS (optional):** Sanity.io or Contentful for easy content updates (decide in Phase 0)
- **Email:** Professional mailboxes via hosting provider, IMAP/SMTP for Outlook
- **Forms:** Netlify Forms, Formspree, or custom API route with email delivery
- **Analytics:** Google Analytics 4 or Plausible (privacy-friendly)

- **Timeline:** Aim for soft launch within 4-6 weeks of kickoff (depends on content approval cycles)
