# PC Quanti MVP Requirements

## Logo Usage Rules

### Branding Guidelines

**Logo design:** PC house icon with blue background (`#2C02D9`)

**Logo usage principles:**

✅ **DO:**
- Keep logo simple and clean (PC house icon)
- Use primary brand color `#2C02D9` (bright blue) in logo
- Ensure "PC Quanti" text is readable in all logo placements
- Maintain adequate whitespace around logo
- Use logo in header (links to home page)
- Keep logo proportions consistent across all pages

❌ **AVOID:**
- Overly detailed or geometric marks that overpower the company name
- Generic arch/house clip-art feel
- Nuclear-specific imagery (stay sector-agnostic across industrial sectors)
- Cluttered logo lockups with too many visual elements
- Making logo dominate the header (keep branding concise)

### Logo Iteration History

**Evolution:** Started with house+arch design → removed arch (too generic/clip-art feel) → settled on simple PC house icon with blue background

**Client preference:** Phil prefers clean, simple branding that doesn't overpower content. Logo should support the professional image without being the dominant visual element.

### Design Context

**Reference:** Logo aligns with overall "crisp" design philosophy (generous whitespace, clean layouts, no "lumpy" cluttered boxes)

**Brand anchor:** The `#2C02D9` blue is a client-approved brand color used consistently in logo and primary CTAs
